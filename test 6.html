Magic Cake

Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes in the world has ever known.Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and these are their stories.
We also need to stress that whatever happens to you as a result of consuming Magic Cake is your responsibility. You've been warned, and you can view our legal page for more information.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 pound of sugar
  • 3 sticks of butter

Instructions

  1. Mix eggs,sugar and butter in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a non-stick plate.
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
  4. Let it rest at room temperature for 20 minutes.
  5. Eat and enjoy!
